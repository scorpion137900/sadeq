Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy ("Policy") outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you provide when using sadeqadvertising.com (the "Website").

We are dedicated to protecting your privacy. If we ask for specific information by which you can be identified while using the Website, rest assured that it will only be used in accordance with this Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name and job title
  • Contact information, including email address
  • Demographic information such as postal code, preferences, and interests
  • Additional information relevant to customer surveys and/or promotional offers

How We Use Information

The information we collect helps us understand your needs and provide better service, specifically for the following purposes:

  • Internal record-keeping
  • Enhancing our products and services
  • Sending occasional promotional emails about new products, special offers, or other information we believe may interest you
  • Contacting you for market research purposes

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your personal information is secure. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, we have implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial measures to protect the information we collect online.

Cookies

We use traffic log cookies to identify which pages are being used on our Website. This helps us analyze web page traffic data and improve the Website, tailoring it to customer needs. We use this information solely for statistical analysis, after which the data is removed from the system. Cookies enable us to enhance your experience by monitoring which pages you find helpful and which you don’t. A cookie does not give us access to your computer or any personal information other than what you choose to share with us.

Controlling Your Personal Information

You can control the collection and use of your personal information in the following ways:

  • Whenever you fill out a form on the Website, look for the option to indicate that you do not wish your information to be used for direct marketing purposes.
  • If you have previously consented to us using your personal information for direct marketing, you can withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at hello@sadeqadvertising.com .

Links to Other Websites

Our Website may contain links to other websites of interest. However, once you leave our site via these links, please note that we do not have control over the other websites. As such, we cannot be responsible for the protection and privacy of any information you provide while visiting these external sites, which are not governed by this Policy. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any linked websites.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time by modifying this page. Please check back periodically to ensure you are satisfied with any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at hello@sadeqadvertising.com .
